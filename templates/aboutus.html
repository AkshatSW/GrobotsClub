About Us!

Grobots Club

Our Journey in Robotics Innovation at SRMCEM, Lucknow

Grobots, the pioneering Robotics Club at Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management (SRMCEM) in Lucknow, commenced its journey in 2013-14. As a hub for innovation, we have been at the forefront of promoting technology education, creating an environment where passion, creativity, and knowledge converge.

Nurturing Innovation and Technology in Our DNA

At Grobots, our mission is to inspire and nurture a deep-seated passion for robotics and technology. Through a myriad of activities, we provide a platform for students to explore their creativity, enhance their technical skills, and cultivate a collaborative spirit within the vast field of robotics.

Unleashing Creativity through Hands-On Learning

Emphasizing hands-on learning, we believe in the power of practical experiences to reinforce theoretical knowledge. From assembling components to programming intricate algorithms, our workshops are designed to bring theoretical concepts to life.

Celebrating Innovation: Our Patents

In our relentless pursuit of excellence, Grobots has achieved patents that symbolize our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what is possible. These patents stand as a testament to the groundbreaking ideas generated by our members.

Extending Our Reach: Community Outreach

Our passion for robotics extends beyond the college campus. Through workshops and classes, we actively engage with schools and communities, introducing students of all ages to the exciting world of technology. By igniting an early interest in robotics, we contribute to the development of future innovators.

A Diverse Community: Join the Grobots Family

Grobots is more than just a club; it's a family of diverse minds coming together to explore, learn, and innovate. Whether you're a novice or an experienced enthusiast, our inclusive community welcomes you. Our team, comprised of both students and accomplished professionals, is dedicated to creating an environment where everyone can learn, grow, and share their love for robotics.

Connecting Innovation: Gantavya, Tech Fests, and Robotics Events

Join us at Gantavya, our annual tech fest, where innovation meets excitement. Explore the latest in robotics technology and witness the brilliant creations of our talented members. Participate in our robotics events, showcasing autonomous robots, Robo Soccer, Robo Race, Mega War, Mini War, and semiautonomous bots.

Tech Innovation Hub: SRMCEM Robotics Club

As a pivotal part of the SRMCEM community, our Robotics Club is a driving force behind technological advancement. We encourage student-led initiatives, emphasize the importance of engineering excellence, and foster an environment where future technology leaders are cultivated.
