Our Story

Welcome to Grobots, the official robotics club of Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management, Lucknow, since 2013-14. We're all about making robotics simple and fun. Through hands-on workshops, we break down complex concepts into easy-to-understand lessons, catering to beginners and enthusiasts alike. Our passion extends to organizing exciting robotics events, from local competitions to big ones like IIT Lucknow. Our college, SRMCEM, has been our strong support. Our accomplished seniors, some of whom have started their own robotics-focused startups, serve as a source of inspiration. Join us at Grobots, where we make robotics accessible to all and celebrate the joy of building and competing together.
